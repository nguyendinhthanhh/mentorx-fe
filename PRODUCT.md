# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Clients, approved mentors, moderators, and administrators using Mentor X to discover expertise, negotiate work, manage contracts and courses, communicate, moderate activity, and operate wallet and escrow workflows. Admin users need dense, trustworthy operational screens for reviewing evidence and taking high-impact actions safely.

## Product Purpose

Mentor X is a professional mentor marketplace with connected user, mentor, moderation, and administration workspaces. Success means users can move from discovery through a funded contract and completion with clear lifecycle, identity, and financial state at every step.

## Brand Personality

Practical, professional, trustworthy. The interface should feel calm and specific to real marketplace work, with clarity taking priority over decoration.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a generic SaaS template, crypto dashboard, Dribbble mockup, decorative admin shell, or fake AI analytics product. Avoid synthetic metrics, placeholder business data, excessive gradients, glowing cards, and workspace hero sections.

## Design Principles

1. Show backend business truth and make the next valid action obvious.
2. Make financial and permission-sensitive states explicit, calm, and auditable.
3. Prefer scan-friendly operational density over decorative whitespace.
4. Keep marketplace, user workspace, MentorHub, and AdminHub boundaries clear.
5. Use honest loading, empty, unavailable, and error states instead of fake completeness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic controls, readable contrast, reduced-motion preferences, and status communication that does not rely on color alone. Product copy must remain available in English and Vietnamese through i18n.
